Overview

The Future AI Conference is a two-day event jointly organised by NVIDIA and Bangor University, exploring emerging trends in artificial intelligence.

Dates and Venue

  • Dates: 4–5 March 2026
  • Venue: Dean Street, Bangor University

Programme Themes

  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Applied AI and real-world deployment
  • AI research across Bangor University
  • Industry perspectives and training from NVIDIA
