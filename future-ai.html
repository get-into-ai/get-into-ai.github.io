Overview

The Future AI Conference is a two-day event jointly organised by NVIDIA and Bangor University, exploring emerging trends in artificial intelligence.

Dates and Venue

  • Dates: 4-5 March 2026
  • Venue: Dean Street, Bangor University

Programme Themes

  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Applied AI and real-world deployment
  • AI research across Bangor University
  • Industry perspectives and training from NVIDIA

Full programme will be posted online shortly.

Wednesday will feature a full-day of speakers and experts from NVIDIA, alongside presentations on current and future developments in AI.

Thursday offers two parallel streams (please choose one when registering):

  • Conference Stream – featuring speakers from across the University showcasing AI research and projects.
  • NVIDIA Training Stream – a full-day specialist LLM training session delivered by NVIDIA (prior programming experience required) and only available for Bangor University staff and students. 45 spaces available.
