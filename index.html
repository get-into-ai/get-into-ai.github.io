About Get Into AI

Get Into AI is a Bangor University initiative focused on AI education, research, and engagement with industry and the wider community.

The project brings together academics, students, and partners to explore current and future developments in artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on responsible, applied, and impactful AI. With the aim to encourage people to consider and learn about AI.

The Future AI Conference

A joint conference with NVIDIA, taking place on 4–5 March 2026 at Bangor University.
